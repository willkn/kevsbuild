Community Consultation 
The Avenue, Campsall


We are seeking Neighbourhood Engagement on our proposals to develop the former Campsall Club. Due to Covid 19 we are unable to carry out our normal Public Consultation process and have instead prepared an online consultation portal to allow feedback & dialogue with local stakeholders. This portal will be open for a period of two weeks and will close on the 14th June 2021.   

Housing are proposing to build 19 family homes on land formally known as the Campsall Club. The site has been derelict for several years and the former club has fallen into disrepair, becoming a focus of antisocial behaviour. The homes we are planning will go some way to address the housing shortage in Doncaster, we have liaised with DMBC to understand the housing need in the area and our proposals for a mix of 2 and 3 bedroomed family homes address this. We are committed to delivering affordable homes for the benifit of the community, our proposals are to provide homes for affordable rent. Together Housing are seeking to engage with the community and obtain your feedback on our proposals prior to finalising our design and submitting for Planning Approval.  If granted we will look to complete the construction of the homes within approximately 1 year of starting construction works.


About Together Housing

We are one of the largest housing associations in the North of England, managing over 36,000 homes across Yorkshire and Lancashire. We are a non-profit organisation, meaning any money we make is invested back into the company for the benefit of our residents and local communities. In addition to providing quality homes to rent, we also build and manage homes for sale through the Shared Ownership scheme. Additionally we provide sheltered accommodation and extra care schemes. Not only do we provide homes, but through our charity, Newground Together, we fund a variety of voluntary and community projects, groups to help improve life and opportunities to work for local people.
